Utah, USA

Utah is a lovely landlocked state in the USA. It boasts a wide variety of environments for all outdoor enthusiasts, from red rock arches and canyons, fresh and saltwater lakes, mountains for hiking, mountain biking, and skiing, and several beautiful pine forests. If you like being outside, there are plenty of options! The larger cities, such as Salt Lake City and Lehi are also a hotbed for tech development, education, and the arts.
